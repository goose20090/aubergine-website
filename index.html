Aubergine Peanut Stew

Ingredients (for 4-5?)

  • 2-3 aubergines (depending on size)
  • 3 onions
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 1-2 tbsp paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp dried thyme
  • 2-3 tins plum tomatoes
    Optional: additional cherry tomatoes to roast in oven and add to sauce in substitue of some tinned plum tomatoes
  • Encona scotch bonnet chilli sauce (1-2tsp? careful with it friends)
  • Veg stock (tsp bouillon, or 1 cube)
  • Salt/pepper (if you have white pepper that is really nice instead of black)
  • Peanut butter (smooth is best)

To Serve

  • Yoghurt (mandatory)
  • Encona (mandatory)
  • Greens (roated broccoli, as we know, is delicious - if you can be arsed with okra that is also great roasted or fried alongside this)
  • Carb (flatbreads/white rice/brown rice)

Method

  1. Chop aubergine and onion into large chunks
  2. (optional)- salt aubergine and leave in colander over sink. This makes it cook easier and more soft/sliky in main dish.
  3. Fry for 15-20 mins, ideally in lots of oil, and adding salt and pepper, until both are browned and softened
  4. Add garlic, paprika, thyme, ground coriander, fry around until aromatic (1-2 mins)
  5. Add tinned tomatoes (I prefer whole plum tomatoes and then squidging them with my hands out of the tin, because I am a child, but normal chopped will do)
